Humidity vs. Latitude


This plot shows the relative humidity at each location.

Most of the countries have humidity higher than 60% and on latitudes over 20 Very few locations have relative humidity below 40%. This could be because of human preference when choosing a location to settle, or more likely because the majority of the planet rarely falls below 30%.

The driest location in the dataset is San Carlos, Nicaragua, with 1% humidity.

The are 18 locations with 100% humidity in this dataset. In the US only there are 7: Kodiak, Winfiled,Los Alamos, Pacific Grove, Salinas, Cedar City and Snyder.
