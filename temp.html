Max Temperature vs. Latitude


This plot shows the max temperature (F) for 9/22/19 at each location.

Max temperature peaks around latitude +20 and trails off considerably towards +90 and -90. This data was collected at the end of summer in the northern hemisphere, which would explain the concentration of high temperatures slightly north of the equator.

The hottest temperature recorded in the data set is 95.99F in Kambar, Pakistan.


Emily Goldner 2019
